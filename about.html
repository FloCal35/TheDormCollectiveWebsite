About

The Dorm Collective is a student-run music group and independent label founded at Belmont University in Fall 2024 by Caleb Flores, Aidan McCarel, Cooper Chu, and Austin Windau. Born out of late-night dorm sessions and a shared desire to grow as artists, the Collective serves as both a collaborative platform and a launchpad for the founders’ individual musical projects.

Functioning as both an artist on streaming platforms and an in-house label, The Dorm Collective exists to explore new sounds, share creative experiments, and publish music without boundaries. With no fixed genre, the group embraces stylistic variety, releasing songs that reflect each member’s unique voice while inviting friends and fellow Belmont students to join the creative process.

Rooted in simplicity, friendship, and a commitment to artistic growth, The Dorm Collective values collaboration and exploration over perfection. Their short-term goal is to build a rich and diverse discography—and in the long run, they hope to see their music played by peers and rising artists across the Belmont scene and beyond.
