About

The Dorm Collective is a student-run music group founded at Belmont University in the Fall of 2024 by Caleb Flores, Aidan McCarel, Cooper Chu, and Austin Windau. Born out of late-night dorm sessions and a shared desire for growth as artists, The Dorm Collective serves as both a collaborative platform and a launchpad for their individual projects.

Functioning as both an artist on streaming platforms and an in-house label, The Dorm Collective exists to explore new sounds, share creative experiments, and publish music without boundaries. With no fixed genre, the group embraces variety, releasing songs that reflect each member’s unique style while inviting friends and fellow Belmont students to join the creative process.

Rooted in simplicity, friendship, and a commitment to artistic growth, The Dorm Collective values collaboration and exploration over perfection of their songs. Their short-term goal is to build a rich and diverse discography—and in the long run, they hope to see their music played by peers and rising artists across the Belmont scene and beyond.
