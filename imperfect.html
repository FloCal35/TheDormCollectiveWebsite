Imperfect


Release date:

January 24, 2025


Genre:

Pop Ballad


About:

Imperfect was the first song by The Dorm Collective, written by Caleb and Aidan during their first semester at Belmont. Recorded and released at the beginning of their second semester, it kickstarted The Dorm Collective’s journey. This heartfelt song explores how flaws can create deep, meaningful relationships. It opens with one person dreaming of an ideal version of themselves and the perfect relationship. In responce, another voice comes and reassures them that these imperfections are what make them human and worthy of love. The final moments of the song emphasize unity and the authenticity of relationships. It ends with shared realization that no one can be everything on their own - we all need someone by our side.
